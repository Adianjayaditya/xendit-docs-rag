# Billease

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image-1735810846058.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A10Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A10Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=d9nwFMIIYy2IauZwFYlM3F%2BVBHzVvr4g4%2BiGC9zBEpE%3D)

BillEase is a buy now, pay later (BNPL) service popular in the Philippines that allows customers to split their purchases into installments. It offers flexible payment options, often with zero interest, making online shopping more accessible to those who may not have credit cards or prefer installment plans.
