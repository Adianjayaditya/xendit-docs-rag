# Appota

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(73).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A31%3A55Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A07%3A55Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=alptE8OCOBuDUSpKJvkS2o1vrhGh%2Fo4Vw4trsxujZD4%3D "image(73).png")

Appota Wallet began as a part of Appota Group, initially focusing on gaming-related payments in Vietnam. In 2015, it expanded into the fintech sector, evolving into a comprehensive mobile wallet offering services such as bill payments, money transfers, and online shopping. Its balance can also be used to purchase gaming credits, integrating its roots with its new financial services.

Appota Wallet’s strong foundation in the gaming community and its seamless payment experience have helped it attract a loyal user base. Its collaborations with various merchants and frequent promotional campaigns have further boosted its popularity across different demographics.
