# Cards

Accepting credit cards is essential for reaching Singapore’s digitally active customers. Shoppers in Singapore rely on cards for everyday spending, online checkout, travel, and cross-border purchases, and they expect a smooth card experience on every site. By enabling card acceptance, you unlock higher conversion, larger order values and global customer reach.\
\
You also gain support for subscriptions, saved cards, refunds, and advanced fraud controls. Enabling cards through our platform ensures you offer the payment method customers trust most, without adding operational complexity to your integration.
