# GrabPay

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/images.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A32%3A32Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A08%3A32Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=f0zcHb4BvRiux43fQLElIIByMJ4RYcnvLSWnoAE9rno%3D)

GrabPay is the digital wallet service of Grab, Southeast Asia's leading super app. In the Philippines, GrabPay has become a trusted and widely used payment method, allowing users to pay for Grab services (like transport, food delivery, and parcels) as well as online and in-store transactions.

GrabPay is known for its seamless integration within the Grab app, enabling a one-stop solution for both payments and everyday services. Beyond Grab’s ecosystem, users can pay bills, shop online, send money, and even earn GrabRewards points. Its security, versatility, and frequent promotions make it a preferred choice for tech-savvy Filipinos.
