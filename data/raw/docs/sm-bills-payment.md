# SM Bills Payment

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image-1736043437256.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A35%3A00Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A11%3A00Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=xJxawh80HhVvdPKzOot2QmFFtXnl5N%2F79TdJAMTkDZ0%3D "image(23).png")

SM Bills Payment counters, located within SM Department Stores, Supermarkets, and Hypermarkets, provide a convenient location for customers to pay for online purchases offline using cash. By presenting a payment reference number from the online transaction, customers can settle their bills with ease.

## How to pay

1. Visit your nearest SM Malls outlet branch before payment code expires
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a Bills Payment (not remittance)
3. You will be given a `BPS Validation Slip` to fill up

   BPS Validation Slip

   Please fill out the following information:

   1. Biller Company: DRAGONPAY
   2. Card/Account/Policy No: Payment Code given by merchant
   3. Date of Payment: Date today
   4. Account Name: Name of Payor
   5. Mode of Payment:

      1. Tick: `Cash`
      2. ₱: Amount due for your payment code
   6. Signature: Payor’s signature\

   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 10.21.04.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A35%3A00Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A11%3A00Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=xJxawh80HhVvdPKzOot2QmFFtXnl5N%2F79TdJAMTkDZ0%3D)
4. Give the filled out slip to the cashier/staff with your payment (cash only).
5. You will receive a receipt as proof of payment\
