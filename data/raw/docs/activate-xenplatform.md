# Activate xenPlatform

To activate xenPlatform, follow these 3 simple steps:

1. Log in to your Xendit dashboard
2. Click **xenPlatform** on your sidebar
3. Click **Enable xenPlatform**
4. Fill in the relevant details about your business and how you will use xenPlatform

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/enable-xp.webp?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A07%3A23Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A18%3A23Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=SIMJgsBb6Va7sar1bnqIkFrLYHmj56cRoPVu8139FhM%3D)

Once you complete this process, xenPlatform will be instantly enabled for you.
