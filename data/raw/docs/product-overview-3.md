# Overview

> Pilot Feature Notice
>
> Bill payments is currently in pilot phase.

**Xendit Bill Payments** enables merchants to facilitate various bill payment services through a single API integration. This service allows your end users to pay utility bills, purchase prepaid services, and make regular payments all within your application or platform.

### What are Bill Payments?

Bill Payments is a digital service that lets people pay their bills and make purchases electronically. Think of it as a digital payment counter where your users can handle multiple types of payments in one place. In Indonesia, this service is commonly known as PPOB (Payment Point Online Banks).

**Common Features**

- **Pay Utility Bills**: Electricity, water, and other utility services
- **Buy Prepaid Services**: Phone credit top-ups, electricity tokens, internet data packages
- **Make Regular Payments**: Government taxes, insurance premiums, school fees

### How It Works?

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(114).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A05%3A04Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A16%3A04Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=6tcEYOTzZjoWD%2F%2F7t2xLWeEVrwlGRfS719%2Bq6GB8R8Q%3D)

1. **Customer provides account information** - The user enters their account number (like electricity customer ID)
2. **System checks bill details** - Xendit verifies the account and retrieves bill information
3. **Customer confirms and pays** - User reviews bill details and completes payment
4. **Payment is processed** - Transaction is executed immediately
5. **Customer receives notification** - Confirmation of successful payment is sent

Instead of visiting multiple service offices to pay different bills, your users can complete all their payments through your platform with Xendit Bill Payments.

### Why Xendit Bill Payments?

- **One Integration, Regional Reach**: Single integration for bill payments across countries.
- **Attractive Commercial Terms**: Process bill payments and collect funds through Xendit's payment methods with zero payment collection fees (for selected payment methods), complemented by bill payment revenue.
- **Leverage Xendit's Ecosystem**: Access comprehensive features from Xendit's payment gateway ecosystem - unified reporting, billing, instant notifications, and reliable uptime backed by 24/7 support.
