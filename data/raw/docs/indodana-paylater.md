# Indodana PayLater

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/indodana-logo.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A28%3A20Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A39%3A20Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=cDl277vl8iQx1y8gGKALPpt98KuOtnFUBkuzqoBi2E8%3D)

Indodana PayLater is a digital credit service that allows users to purchase goods or services on credit at various merchants partnered with Indodana Finance.
