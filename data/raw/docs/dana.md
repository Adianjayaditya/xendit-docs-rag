# DANA

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(51).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A28%3A11Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A41%3A11Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=69O3ZODBfyLYiUpQBBh9jK3M8NY3YEEtozAQGIvf8HQ%3D "image(51).png")

DANA is a versatile digital payment platform that has become one of Indonesia's most popular e-wallets. DANA is a homegrown fintech company backed by Ant Group and has established strong partnerships, serving millions of Indonesian users with its comprehensive digital financial services.

When customers choose DANA at checkout, they can complete the payment either by logging into their DANA account directly on your checkout page, or by opening their DANA app and authorizing the payment through the app's interface. The payment is confirmed instantly once the customer verifies the transaction using their DANA PIN or biometric authentication.
