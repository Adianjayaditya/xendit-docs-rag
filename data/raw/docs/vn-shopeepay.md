# ShopeePay

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(62).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A32%3A04Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A08%3A04Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=z73bnJAamq7Z4I7GbU9545W7mY1wBYrwyA6h8sq2pco%3D "image(62).png")

ShopeePay is an e-wallet service offered by Shopee, one of the largest e-commerce platforms in Southeast Asia. It allows customers to make fast and secure payments for online and offline transactions. ShopeePay's growing user base in Indonesia makes it a convenient and widely trusted payment option.

When customers choose ShopeePay at checkout, they can complete the payment by scanning a QR code or authorizing the transaction directly in the Shopee app. Once the payment is completed, you receive instant confirmation, ensuring a smooth and efficient transaction process for your business.
