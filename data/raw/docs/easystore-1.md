# EasyStore

Easystore is a popular e-commerce platform that allows businesses to easily set up and manage their online stores. It offers customizable storefronts, order management tools, and integrations to help merchants grow their business.

To make payments seamless, the **Xendit EasyStore Plugin** enables you to integrate Xendit’s payment gateway directly into your Easystore store. With just a few configurations, you can start accepting a wide range of payment methods supported by Xendit.
