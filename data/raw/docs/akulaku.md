# Akulaku

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/akulaku-logo.svg?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A27%3A30Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A03%3A30Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=tCDlyG0O8wL2YI7trzaVy1rAdABomsKvgnnGXWzvEqY%3D)Akulaku is a leading banking and digital finance platform in Southeast Asia, with a strong presence in Indonesia. Founded in 2016, Akulaku started as a consumer lending platform and has since expanded its services to include e-commerce, digital banking, and wealth management. Akulaku aims to provide accessible financial services to underserved customers in emerging markets, particularly those who lack access to traditional banking.

The platform offers a range of products, including virtual credit cards, installment loans, and online shopping, all accessible through its mobile app. Akulaku's innovative approach to financial services has made it a popular choice for Indonesians seeking convenient and affordable financial solutions.
