# Kredivo

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/kredivo-logo.svg?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A27%3A18Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A03%3A18Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=p6l94xvkx1MswuDSHJn%2FOXiGhW9cSFMQpoAINZVJaL0%3D)

Kredivo is a leading digital credit platform in Indonesia, offering instant credit for e-commerce, offline purchases, and personal loans through real-time decisioning. Customers can choose Kredivo as a PayLater payment option.

## Payment flow

1. On the checkout page, select **PayLater > Kredivo**
2. Fill in customer details and continue
3. Choose installment plan on the Kredivo payment page
4. Log in to Kredivo with your phone number and PIN, or create a new account
5. Enter the OTP sent to your phone and click **Bayar** (Pay)
