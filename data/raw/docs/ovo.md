# OVO

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(49).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A28%3A43Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A04%3A43Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=H7qQI9yX6VjzzyROaezxd7aZ1IyoblUM%2Bf3tr7zXROI%3D "image(49).png")

OVO is one of Indonesia's leading digital payment and financial services platforms. It’s a prominent e-wallet service backed by Grab and has established itself as a preferred payment method for millions of Indonesians, particularly integrated with popular services like Tokopedia and Grab.

When customers select OVO at checkout, they can complete the payment through two methods: by logging into their OVO account directly on your checkout page, or by opening their OVO app and authorizing the payment through the app's interface. The payment is confirmed instantly once the customer approves the transaction using their OVO PIN or biometric authentication.
