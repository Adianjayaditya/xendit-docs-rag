# Features

> Use our new [Payment Session features](/v1/docs/how-payment-sessions-work#payment-link) and see our [migration guide page here](/v1/docs/migrate-to-payment-session)

Xendit's Payment Links come with powerful features that let you tailor the checkout experience for your customers. You can control everything from the payment channels offered to the look and feel of the checkout page, while automated notifications and built-in conversion tracking help you stay informed and optimize your sales process.

| **Feature** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| [Configure payment channels](/docs/customize-xendit-hosted-page) | Manage payment channels displayed on the checkout UI, either per link or as a default setting. |
| [Customize Xendit-hosted page](/docs/customize-checkout-page) | Align the Xendit-hosted page appearance with your business identity by customizing colors and layouts. |
| [Notifications](/v1/docs/send-notifications-to-customer) | Automate customer notifications for payment statuses and reminders via email or WhatsApp. |
| [Conversion tracking](/v1/docs/conversion-tracking) | Track conversion events using Facebook Pixel and Google Analytics to optimize your marketing efforts. |
| [Card BIN whitelist](/v1/docs/cards-bin-whitelist) | The Xendit-hosted page can be configured to accept payments only from specific BINs (Bank Identification Numbers) as defined during Payment Link creation.  *This feature is available exclusively for Payment Links created via API integration.* |
