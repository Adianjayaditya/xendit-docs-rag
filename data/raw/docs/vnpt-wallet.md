# VNPT Wallet

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(75).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A32%3A09Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A08%3A09Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=5NB1wbF2hVt%2BOWpzI4FiB%2B%2FS8UXZnN8t3reQS%2FGc9IU%3D "image(75).png")

VNPT Wallet was developed by VNPT Group, a leading telecommunications provider in Vietnam, as part of its digital transformation strategy. It offers a range of financial services, including bill payments, mobile top-ups, money transfers, and QR code transactions. The wallet is integrated with VNPT’s ecosystem, allowing seamless access to its other digital services, such as e-government and enterprise solutions.

With VNPT’s strong brand and extensive customer base, VNPT Wallet has quickly gained traction. Its user-friendly interface, secure payment processes, and collaborations with merchants and service providers have made it a popular choice among individuals and businesses in Vietnam.
