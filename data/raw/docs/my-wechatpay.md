# WeChat Pay

WeChat is a comprehensive app used in China for instant messaging, social media, and mobile payments. Users with Chinese bank accounts can link these accounts to their WeChat profiles, enabling them to conduct payments online and in person through WeChat Pay.

WeChat Pay boasts over 900 million active monthly users, making it an essential payment option for businesses targeting Chinese consumers. You can integrate WeChat Pay into your website for both desktop and mobile browser users. Our integration tools, such as Drop-in or Component, include a WeChat Pay button on your checkout page.

## Payment flow

For **desktop users,** a QR code is displayed on the payment page, which they can scan using their WeChat Pay app to complete the transaction. This functionality is readily available when you add WeChat Pay to your Customer Area.

**Mobile users** are redirected to their WeChat Pay app to finalize their payment, after which they are sent back to your payment page.
