# ECPay Loans

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Ecpay Loans Image.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D "image(23).png")

ECPay Loans facilitates loan repayments for various lending companies through its diverse payment channels, including Maya Bills and GCash Bills. By using ECPay Loans, lenders can offer borrowers flexible and convenient repayment options, improving loan collection efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## How to pay

Maya Bills

1. Open your PayMaya App
2. Tap the “Bills” button\
   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 16.47.25.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
3. Search for “Dragon Loans” using the search button located at the upper left of the screen\
   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.00.46.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
4. Fill in the required details. Please fill out the following information:

   - Account Number: Payment code you given by merchant
   - Amount: Amount due of your payment code
   - Mobile Number: Your personal mobile number\
     \
     ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.01.03.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
5. Click “Continue” after filling out the form, you will then be redirected to the confirmation page\
6. Click “Pay” on the confirmation page, you will then be redirected to the processing page

   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.01.13.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
7. Page will be automatically updated after payment is processed and Done

GCash Bills

1. Open your GCash app
2. Tap the “Pay Bills” button\
   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 16.54.16.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
3. Search for “Dragon Loans” using the search button located at the upper left of the screen\
   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.03.19.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
4. Fill in the required details. Please fill out the following information:

   - Reference Number: Payment code you given by merchant
   - Contact Number: Your personal mobile number
   - Amount: Amount due of your payment code\
     ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.03.53.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
5. Click “Confirm” after filling out the form, you will then be redirected to the confirmation page.\
   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.04.16.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
6. Click on “confirm”, you will be redirected to an e-receipt page after payment is successful. Please note that GCash charges end users an additional convenience fee PHP 10 on top of the amount due.

   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-05 at 17.04.51.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A31Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A31Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=Kr89FU0I0nPeVuvWneSzGA4bc9UyP2gQ8PR8nDSvG1I%3D)
