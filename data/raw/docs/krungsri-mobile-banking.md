## Payment flow

[null](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/BAY%20Mobile%20Banking.mp4?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A35%3A24Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A35%3A24Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=DAbqj9bG5hABiX6%2FjJgUSNLUTAUPJXZaI10aZt8SSD4%3D)

1. On checkout page, end customer selects Krungsri Mobile Banking
2. End customer will be redirected to Bank web portal
3. Payment request push notification will be sent to end customer’s Krungsri Mobile Banking app
4. End customers opens Krungsri Mobile Banking & click on the payment notification
5. Bank app shows the payment details and end customer click confirm to complete the payment
