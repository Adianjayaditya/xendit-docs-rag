## How to pay

**Find the QR Code**

1. Visit your nearest Palawan Pawnshop or Palawan Express Pera Padala branch before payment code expires. You may refer to this [branch locator](https://www.palawanpawnshop.com/branch-finder) \*
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a Bills Payment (not a

   remittance)
3. You will be given a `Send Money Form` to fill-up

   Send Money Form

   Please fill out the following information:

   - Sender

     - Name: Your Name
     - Mobile No.: Your mobile number
   - Receiver

     - Name: DRAGONPAY / Payment Code given by merchant
     - Amount: Amount due of your payment code
     - Purpose of Transaction: Payment
     - Relationship to Receiver: Client

   ![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-01-02 at 16.40.29.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A52Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A10%3A52Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=siYEjfGvrVBcS1THRR2l8Lgz6ZQRjFwBC4sMeFVRIMM%3D)
4. Give the filled out slip to the cashier/staff with your payment (cash only)
5. You will receive a receipt as proof of payment

> Note
>
> \*: Pera Padala Authorized Agents only are not yet supported
