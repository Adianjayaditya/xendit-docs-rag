# Overview

Xendit is deeply integrated to local clearing systems across 6 SEA countries and 300+ destination channels via direct local partner integrations to ensure the best experience for you in sending money across, and is continuing to expand its coverage. Local payouts are faster and more cost-effective compared to SWIFT transfers.

\*For cross border payout use cases, each destination currency may have different sender/recipient data requirements. For more information, click the country-specific page in the table below.
