# CVM

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/CVM Pawnshop Home.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A22Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A45%3A22Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=83GEepsqnbSz%2FSz6eRcdyYHI4y8I2IYEk%2F6PU0nERD4%3D "image(23).png")

CVM Pawnshop, with its extensive network of branches across the Philippines, enables businesses to accept over-the-counter payments from customers who prefer offline transactions. Customers can settle online purchases by providing a transaction reference number at any CVM branch, making it a convenient option for those without bank accounts or who favor cash payments.

## How to pay

1. Visit your nearest CVM pawnshop branch before payment code expires.
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a Dragonpay Payment
3. You will be asked to write on a piece of paper these following details:

   1. Your Name
   2. Your Mobile Number
   3. Your Birthday
   4. Payment Code given by Merchant
   5. Amount
   6. Purpose of Payment
4. Give the filled out paper to the cashier/staff with your payment (cash only)
5. You will receive a receipt as proof of payment\
