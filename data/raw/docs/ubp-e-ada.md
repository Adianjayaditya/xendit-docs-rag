# UnionBank eADA

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Unionbank-logo.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A35%3A08Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A11%3A08Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=O1cYS%2BAfqr2ynz6n3PuwLPtveGIoLyS80BIK9PFqsxM%3D)

On top of regular one-time payments with UnionBank direct debit, Xendit also offers UnionBank Auto Debit Arrangement (also know as ADA or e-ADA) which enables businesses to automatically collect recurring payments directly from their customers’ UnionBank deposit accounts. This payment solution is widely used for automating bills, subscription fees, loan payments, and other scheduled transactions.

UnionBank ADA is highly regarded for its convenience, security, and efficiency. For customers, it eliminates the hassle of manually paying recurring bills, while businesses benefit from timely and reliable payment collection.
