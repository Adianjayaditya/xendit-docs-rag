# FPX Online Banking

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(162).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A31%3A41Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A42%3A41Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=58jjt2mZs8Qp8T15%2BrhVYmjKwq%2BEo9bDOtZ7B9cBidg%3D)

FPX is an online payment service that allows customers to pay for purchases and bills in real-time by debiting their bank account directly through their internet banking portal. It is a secure and reliable payment gateway, operated by PayNet, a subsidiary of Bank Negara Malaysia, that enables secure and direct crediting to the merchant's account.

## Payment flow

Step 1: Select FPX Online banking and choose the required bank.\
\
![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/image(198).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A31%3A41Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A42%3A41Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=58jjt2mZs8Qp8T15%2BrhVYmjKwq%2BEo9bDOtZ7B9cBidg%3D)

Step 2: You will be redirected to the chosen bank online banking page. Login with banking credentials (example Maybank).

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-11-14 at 2.42.47 pm(1).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A31%3A41Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A42%3A41Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=58jjt2mZs8Qp8T15%2BrhVYmjKwq%2BEo9bDOtZ7B9cBidg%3D)

Step 3: Select the account, click “Continue” and complete the 3DS (example Maybank).\
\
![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screenshot 2025-11-14 at 2.43.30 pm.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A31%3A41Z&se=2026-04-23T06%3A42%3A41Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=58jjt2mZs8Qp8T15%2BrhVYmjKwq%2BEo9bDOtZ7B9cBidg%3D)

Step 4: Payment completed.
