## Payment flow

[null](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/TH%20Kbank%20MB.mp4?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A35%3A20Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A35%3A20Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=5tnWBOxwMFMxa7%2BpSe19ExyWVUYruQQ%2FXWlv%2BO8MJ9I%3D)

1. On checkout page, end customer selects KBank Mobile Banking
2. End customer will be redirected to Bank web portal.
3. End customer input mobile number & code to proceed
4. End customer check payment details & click ‘continue’ to confirm
5. Payment request notification will be sent to end customer’s KBank Mobile Banking app
6. End customers opens KBank Mobile Banking & click on the payment notification
7. Bank app shows the payment details and end customer click confirm to complete the payment
