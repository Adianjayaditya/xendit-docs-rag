# Robinsons Bills Payment

![](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Robinsons Department Store Logo 2013.svg?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-04-23T06%3A34%3A56Z&se=2026-04-23T07%3A10%3A56Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=sXuk%2Brk68k3cHOUMi4iCEwhO4ViOYE1pCjR0UiKj5H8%3D "image(23).png")

Robinsons Bills Payment, offered through their Business Centers, enables customers to settle online transactions offline using cash. By providing a reference number, customers can conveniently pay for their online purchases at any Robinsons Department Store.

## How to pay

1. Visit your nearest Robinsons Business Center (Robinsons malls department stores) before payment code expires
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a Bills Payment (not remittance)
3. You will be given a `Payment Form` to fill up

   Payment Form

   Please fill out the following information:

   1. Account Name: [Name of Payor]
   2. Contact No: [Payor’s contact number]
   3. Date of Payment: [Date today]
   4. Biller Company: DRAGONPAY
   5. Card/Account/Policy/Reference No.: [Payment Code given by merchant]
   6. Total Payment: ₱ [Amount due for your payment code]
   7. Signature
4. Give the filled out slip to the cashier/staff with your payment (cash only)
5. You will receive a receipt as proof of payment\
